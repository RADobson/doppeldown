## Product Overview

Netcraft is the oldest player in anti-phishing and brand protection. Originally an internet research company (famous for the Netcraft Web Server Survey), they pivoted to cybercrime disruption. Acquired by Spectrum Equity PE in 2023 ‚Äî signaling growth ambitions.

### Core Capabilities
- **Phishing site detection** ‚Äî Industry-leading, processes for all major browsers
- **Domain monitoring** ‚Äî Comprehensive typosquatting and lookalike detection
- **Takedown services** ‚Äî Industry-leading automated takedown with ~24hr average
- **Certificate Transparency monitoring** ‚Äî Strong CT log analysis
- **Malicious site blocking** ‚Äî Feeds to browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari safe browsing)
- **App monitoring** ‚Äî Fake app detection in app stores
- **Email fraud detection** ‚Äî DMARC-based protection

### Key Differentiators
- **Takedown speed** ‚Äî Claims fastest takedown times in the industry
- **Browser integration** ‚Äî Their data feeds into safe browsing databases (massive distribution)
- **30-year data moat** ‚Äî Decades of internet infrastructure data
- **Government contracts** ‚Äî UK government and financial sector relationships
- **Free tools** ‚Äî Site report, extension, and some free scanning tools
