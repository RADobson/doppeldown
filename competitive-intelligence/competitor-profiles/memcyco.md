## Product Overview

Memcyco takes a unique approach: instead of just detecting phishing sites, they provide **real-time protection for end-users** who land on phishing sites. They embed a "digital watermark" in legitimate sites that's absent from fakes, enabling real-time alerts to end users.

### Core Capabilities
- Real-time website impersonation detection
- Digital watermark verification technology
- End-user phishing alerts (victims see warnings in real-time)
- Brand impersonation monitoring
- Credential exposure detection

### Key Differentiator: Real-Time End-User Protection
Most DRP tools protect the brand. Memcyco also protects the brand's *customers* by alerting them in real-time when they're on a phishing page. This is a genuinely different approach.
