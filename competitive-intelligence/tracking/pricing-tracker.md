## Pricing Strategy Recommendations

### Maintain Current Position If:
- [ ] No Tier 1 competitor launches self-serve

- [ ] Customer acquisition remains strong
- [ ] Win rate vs. enterprise vendors >50%

- [ ] Competitor launches comparable self-serve at <$500/mo
- [ ] Customer feedback indicates price too low (credibility concern)
